Chicken Tomato Pesto Burrata Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 Oz Linguine
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Tsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 1/2 Cups Grape/Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Pepper
  • 3/4 Cup Pesto
  • 2 Cups Cooked Chicken (Shredded or Cubed)
  • 1 Cup Fresh Basil (1 Tbsp dried)
  • 1 Ball Burrata (~4oz)

Directions

  1. Cook and drain the pasta, according to directions.
  2. In a separate pan, heat up the oil and garlic over medium heat.
  3. Add the tomatoes, salt, and pepper, cooking until they begin to soften (about 7-10 minutes).
  4. Set the tomatoes aside.
  5. Add in the pesto and pasta, mixing evenly.
  6. Add in the cooked chicken, mixing until everything is evenly warm.
  7. Add the tomatoes back in along with the fresh basil.
  8. Toss the pasta and remove from heat.
  9. Top with burrata, serve, and enjoy!

Yield: 4 Servings
